# **App Name**: SweetPrice

## Core Features:

- Ingredient Registration: Register ingredients with their purchase costs, units, and suppliers.
- Recipe Builder: Combine registered ingredients to create recipes and automatically calculate the total cost of the product.
- Profit Margin Definition: Set profit margins (percentage or fixed value) to be applied on the total cost of the recipe.
- Suggested Selling Price: Automatically calculate and suggest a selling price based on ingredient costs and desired profit margin.
- Currency Conversion: The app can use a tool to offer the user suggested amounts for equivalent ingredients depending on the availability on other markets.
- Product Cost Analysis: Visual representation of how costs breakdown for ingredients used in the app

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft pink (#F4B4C5) to evoke sweetness and delicateness.
- Background color: Light cream (#F8F5ED) to create a warm and inviting canvas.
- Accent color: Pastel yellow (#F9E79F) to add a touch of brightness and highlight key elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font, for a harmonious blend of aesthetics and functionality.
- Use custom icons with a hand-drawn style that symbolize ingredients and recipe steps.
- Clean and simple layout to provide the best UX, with an ingredient list at the top.
- Subtle animations such as ingredient flying when the cost has changed.
